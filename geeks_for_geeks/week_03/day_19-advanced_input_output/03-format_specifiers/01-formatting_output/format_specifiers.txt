%d or %i: Signed integer
%u: Unsigned integer
%f: Floating-point number (default format)
%e or %E: Floating-point in scientific notation
%g or %G: Floating-point in either fixed or scientific notation (shorter format)
%c: Single character
%s: String
%p: Pointer address
%%: Literal % character
